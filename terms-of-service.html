Terms & Conditions – Bokopano

1. Introduction

Bokopano is a digital platform that connects individuals seeking volunteer or work-exchange opportunities with hosts offering accommodation, skills development, and experience-based opportunities across Botswana and Southern Africa.

2. Platform Role

Bokopano acts solely as an intermediary platform. We do not employ, recruit, or directly manage hosts or volunteers. All arrangements are made independently between users.

3. Eligibility

Users must:

  • be at least 18 years old
  • provide accurate and truthful information
  • use the platform lawfully

4. User Responsibilities

All users agree to:

  • behave respectfully
  • communicate honestly
  • comply with local laws

5. Host Responsibilities

Hosts must:

  • provide accurate information in listings
  • deliver the benefits promised (e.g. accommodation, meals)
  • maintain a safe and respectful environment
  • not exploit volunteers

6. Volunteer Responsibilities

Volunteers must:

  • follow agreed schedules
  • respect host rules
  • not engage in illegal or harmful behavior

7. Nature of Exchange

Opportunities listed on Bokopano are based on work exchange, not formal employment, unless explicitly stated by the parties involved.

8. Safety and Risk

Users participate at their own risk. Bokopano does not guarantee safety, conditions, or outcomes of any arrangement.

9. Limitation of Liability

Bokopano is not liable for:

  • disputes between users
  • personal injury
  • financial loss
  • travel or accommodation issues

10. Account Suspension

We reserve the right to suspend or remove accounts that:

  • provide false information
  • engage in harmful behavior
  • violate these terms

11. Changes

Bokopano may update these terms at any time.
