Privacy Policy – Bokopano

1. Introduction

Bokopano is committed to protecting user data and privacy in accordance with applicable data protection principles within Southern Africa.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • personal details (name, email, phone)
  • profile and listing information
  • uploaded documents
  • usage data
  • device/browser data

3. How We Use Data

We use data to:

  • operate the platform
  • connect hosts and volunteers
  • improve user experience
  • ensure safety and prevent fraud
  • comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with:

  • service providers (hosting, storage)
  • payment providers (future integration)
  • authorities if legally required

5. Data Retention

Data is stored only as long as necessary for:

  • platform operation
  • legal compliance
  • dispute resolution

6. User Rights

Users may:

  • access their data
  • request corrections
  • request deletion of their account

7. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect data, but no system is completely secure.

8. Cookies

We may use cookies for:

  • authentication
  • analytics
  • improving performance

9. Regional Considerations (Southern Africa)

Bokopano operates in regions where data protection laws may vary. We aim to follow best practices aligned with:

  • user consent
  • transparency
  • responsible data handling

10. Changes

This policy may be updated periodically.
